CGM Barriers & Facilitators – FQHC Study

Diabetes Care Access Study  ·  Datagain Services

Led systematic qualitative coding of 12 focus group transcripts on barriers and facilitators to Continuous Glucose Monitor use in Federally Qualified Health Centers. Two-coder IRR protocol achieved over 80% agreement.

Insurance prior authorization, limited vendor support, and low health literacy emerged as compounding barriers — diabetes educators and family support were the strongest facilitators of CGM adoption.
